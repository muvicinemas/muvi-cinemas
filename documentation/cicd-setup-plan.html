üìã The Plan in Plain English

Vendor's buildspec.yml (Reconstructed)

version: 0.2

phases:
  pre_build:
    commands:
      - echo Logging in to Amazon ECR...

      - COMMIT_HASH=$(echo $CODEBUILD_RESOLVED_SOURCE_VERSION | cut -c 1-7)

  build:
    commands:
      - echo Building Docker image...

  post_build:
    commands:
      - echo Pushing to ECR...
